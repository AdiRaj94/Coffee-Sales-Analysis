# Coffee-Sales-Analysis
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse sales performance for a coffee retailer. This project transforms raw transaction data into actionable insights, focusing on revenue trends, product performance and customer behaver.  **Objectives**
  Analyse overall sales performance.
  Identify top and bottom performing products.
  Understand time based sales trends.
  Apply Pareto analysis to identify key revenue drivers.
  Generate actionable business insights.

**Tools & Technologies:**
  Power BI – Data Visualization.
  Dax - Measures and Calculation.
  Excel – Data Preparation.

**KPI:**
  Total Revenue, 
  Total Orders, 
  Average Order Value 

**Dashboard Pages -**                                                                                                            
**Sales Overview:**
Monthly revenue trends show peaks in March and October.
Weekday sales dominates by 75%.
Card payments account for majority of transactions as it is used by 97% customers.                                       
**Product Performance Analysis:**
    Top 5 products generate the majority of revenue
    Latte and Americano with Milk are top performers
    Espresso is the lowest-performing product                                                                               
  **Time Analysis:**
    Pear hours: 8 – 10 AM and 3-5PM
    Highest revenue day: Tuesday
    Lowest sales observed on Sunday                                                                                                  
**Pareto Analysis:**
    Applied 80/20 rule to identify that 5 products contribute to 84% of the total revenue.

**Business Insights:**
Top 5 products – Latte, Americano with milk, Cappuccino, Americano and Hot Chocolate generate 84% revenue. Focus on marketing top performing products and improve low performing products through promotions.

Hot chocolate is a “Premium performer”. It ranks in the top 5 revenue but is in the bottom 3 for quantity sold which suggests that “Hot Chocolate” has high price point and high margin. You don’t need to sell a lot to make a lot. Consider a premium pairing – Hot chocolate and pastry to increase the average order value without needing to find new customer.

Increase experienced staffs to the Tuesday morning shift to handle the volume and maintain speed of service. Since, sunday is slow consider “Sunday family bundle” or “Weekend Loyalty perk” to fill the revenue gap during the weekend slump.

97% customers paid via card which means customer base is tech ready. So, digital means can be used to convert customer rate in afternoon by sending push notification through email.
